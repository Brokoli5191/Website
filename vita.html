Personal Data

name: Roland Joseph Steinbauer
academic titles: Mag. rer. nat., Dr. rer. nat.
current position: Associate Professor (Ao. Univ. Prof.)
born: February, 23, 1970
nationality: Austria legal status: married, two children



Education

1976-80: primary school in Vienna
1980-88: secondary school in Vienna, final examination (“Matura”) passed with honors
Oct.88-May 89: military service
Oct. 89-June 91: undergraduate study of physics and mathematics at the University of Vienna
Oct. 91-Jan.96: graduate study of physics and mathematics at the University of Vienna
Jan. 29, 96: Diploma (theoretical physics) with honors
Mar. 96-Aug. 2000: Ph.D. studies at the University of Vienna
Aug 14, 2000: Ph.D. (mathematics) with honors
Mar 12, 2001: promotio sub auspiciis praesidentis rei publicae
May 15, 2003: habilitation (mathematics) at the University of Vienna



Scientific career

Oct. 92-Jan. 93: Teaching Assistant (Tutor), Department of Mathematics, University of Vienna
Jan. 93-June 93: Teaching Position (Stud. Ass.), parttime, Department of Mathematics, University of Vienna
Sep. 93-Jan. 94: Teaching Assistant (Tutor), Department of Mathematics, University of Vienna
Nov. 96-Apr. 98: Ph.D. scholar of the Austrian Academy of Science
Oct. 97-Jan 98: Teaching Assistant (Tutor), Institute of Theoretical Physics, University of Vienna
May 98-Okt. 99: Scientific Coworker, project P12023MAT of the Austrian Science Foundation (FWF)
July 98-Okt. 99: Teaching Position (Vertragsassistent), parttime Department of Mathematics, University of Vienna
Nov. 99-June 01: Teaching Position (V. Ass.), fulltime Department of Mathematics, University of Vienna
June 01-Sep. 01: Scientific Coworker, project P12023MAT of the Austrian Science Foundation (FWF), research stay at the Faculty of Mathematcial Studies of the University of Southampton (UK)
Sep. 01-Sep. 03: Assistant Professor (Univ. Ass.), Department of Mathematics, University of Vienna
since Oct. 03: Associate Professor (Ao. Univ. Prof.), Department of Mathematics, University of Vienna
